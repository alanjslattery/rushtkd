Welcome Back

The Instructors would like to welcome everybody back after the Christmas holidays. The Calendar of events for 2018 has been updated, to include all the upcoming events that Rush TKD plan to participate in. All students are encouraged to attend as many of these events as possible. We are looking forward to a another great year ahead!

Christmas 2017

The Instructors of Rush Taekwon-Do School would like to wish all our students and their families a very Happy & Peaceful Christmas. Rush TKD has closed for the Holidays and will re-open on Tuesday 9th January 2018.

Students of the Year 2017

Congratulations to Erin, Aoife, Thomas & Eamonn who were awarded our Student of the Year Awards for 2017. Hard work and regular training really pays off.

Merit Award Winners

Congratulations to all our winners Eli, James & Adam. Well done all.

Winter Grade with Grand Master O’Toole

Congratulations to all students who graded on Friday 15th December under Grand Master O’Toole. 23 students presented themselves for all grades up to blue belt. All students performed exceptionally well and Grand Master O’Toole was delighted with the standard shown on the night. Congratulations to all. New belts and tags will be given out on Tuesday our final class before the Christmas holidays.

Rush TKD Winter Grades

Congratulations to all students who graded on Tuesday 12th December with the club instructors. 35 students presented themselves for grading with many stepping on to the mats for their very first grades. There were some very good performances and overall a good standard on the night. Congratulations to all. New belts and tags will be given out on Tuesday our final class before the Christmas holidays.

Black Belt Grading December 2017

Immensely proud day for Rush at the INTA Black Belt Grading in City West Hotel today2nd December. Days like this make it all worthwhile. Big congrats to Mark who achieved his 1st Degree Black Belt.

Grand Master O’Toole’s Visit

It was a great honour to host the first official Grand Master class for the INTA on Saturday 28th October 2017 with GM Brendan O'Toole following on from his recent promotion to the rank of Grand Master at the World Campionships 2017 at City West September 2017. Grand Master O’Toole has is our instructor and mentor for the last 30 years. Thanks to all 26 participants for taking part and making it a very enjoyable occasion.

Summer Grading with Master O’Toole

Rush’s Summer Grade was conducted on Friday 23rd June by Master Brendan O’Toole. There were some excellent tkd skills displayed on the night and the theory questions were very impressive. The instructors were very pleased with the efforts on all our students and a big congratulations to our beginners stepping onto the mats for the first times.

Summer Grading

Rush’s Summer Grade took place on Tuesday 20th June and was conducted by the club instructors. Well done to all students who took part. We had some excellent performances and generally a very good standard. Results will be given out next week in all classes.

Club Championships 2017

On Tuesday the 6th June 2017 Rush TKD held our Club Championships for 2017. Rush TKD would like to thank our judge on the night Mr Paul Floody from the Legacy Club in Drogheda. Well done to all the participants and the instructors were feeling very proud tonight.

About Rush Tae Kwon-Do

Rush Tae Kwon-Do was established in 2007 by Ms Lucia McCarthy (4th Degree) and Ms Ann McCarthy (4th Degree)

All our instructors are fully certified Blackbelts, International Instructors, Garda Vetted, certified in First Aid and Child Protection
