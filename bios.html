About Rush Tae Kwon-Do

Rush Tae Kwon-Do was established in 2007 by Ms Lucia McCarthy (4th Degree) and Ms Ann McCarthy (4th Degree)

All our instructors are fully certified Blackbelts, International Instructors, Garda Vetted, certified in First Aid and Child Protection
