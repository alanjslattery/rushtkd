Welcome to Rush Taekwon-Do School Website

Rush Taekwon-Do School is a community based club situated in Rush National School, Channel Road Rush. The club has separate adult and children classes which are suitable for men, women and children of all ages and fitness levels. Rush Taekwon-Do also has a Taekwon-Do Kids programme exclusively for children 4 to 6 years. Classes are ongoing and beginners are always welcome.

Rush Taekwon-Do School are members of the Irish National Taekwon-Do Association (INTA) who are affiliated to the International Taekwon-Do federation (ITF) and the All European Taekwon-Do Association (AETF).

This website will provide regular updated information in relation to Rush Taekwon-Do School including events, club news, INTA news, upcoming local, national and international events.

All our instructors are ITF certified black belts, Garda vetted, First aid and Child protection certified. Please enjoy our website
